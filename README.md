## 🖥️ What is pdfdelta?

pdfdelta helps you compare two versions of a PDF document side by side. It highlights what was added and what was deleted. This is useful for academic papers, reports, and other documents that change over time.

Instead of just showing text differences, it marks changes directly on the pages where they appear. You get two new PDF files:
- One shows what was deleted from the old version.
- The other shows what was added in the new version.

This visual approach makes it easier to understand complex changes, especially in papers with multiple columns and charts.
